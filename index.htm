max188's European Air War Add-ons

  Hi-res terrain and other mods for EAW and First Eagles


If you want the ultimate WWII virtual air combat experience, look no further than European Air War.  Already a WWII flight sim classic, it continues to get even better with age.  Straight out of the box, EAW is an excellent flight sim.  But, with the ongoing contributions and cooperation of the EAW community, it has become a true masterpiece.  

This site features my enhancements for European Air War.  I hope you like them and get as much enjoyment out of EAW as I do.  Thanks to all those in the EAW community who have shared their skills, knowledge, and time to help make such mods possible.

If you're just starting out with EAW, or getting back in to it after a while away, take a look at the Classic EAW Guide.  This guide lists recommended add-ons you can use to as a good starting point in building out a basic EAW installation, as well as common issues you may encounter.  Also be sure to check out the Desert Air War add-on for a completely new EAW experience!
