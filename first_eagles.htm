First Eagles Downloads
click on any image for an enlarged view

A new WWI flight simulator has recently been released by Third Wire called First Eagles.  Third Wire is headed by none other than Tsuyoshi Kawahito (TK), who was the driving force behind European Air War.  So, if you've enjoyed EAW, I suggest you take a look at First Eagles as a great combat flight simulator recreating the WWI air battles over France.  The game has been released in Europe and will be coming to the U.S. in box form soon.  However, you can download a copy now through Third Wire's online store here.

 

Hi-Res Summer Terrain Set for Third Wire's First Eagles (Updated 1/31/07)

       

This is a hi-res terrain set for First Eagles.  It replaces all of the default terrain with repainted 512x512 tiles (most of the default is 256x256). This terrain set preserves the original locations of the trees and fields, and leverages some features of the original terrain. The result is a landscape that still has a similar feel as the stock terrain, but with much greater detail and visual interest.
