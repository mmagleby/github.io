Sky Manager 2001 by Spit9 (Updated 11/22)  

Sky Manager is a weather utility by Spit9 that manages sky and cloud files.  It comes bundled with several different weather schemes by various artists and allows you to choose between them.  Also allows you to choose random weather or the game's default.  You can also easily add other sky and cloud files as well.
